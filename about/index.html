I'm Eric! I like music, coding and computer science, and running. This site is a place for me to post my thoughts on the stuff I enjoy.

Music

I've been playing guitar for about eleven years; bass on-and-off for about two. I like to think I can sort of sing, and I can find my way around a keyboard with some level of proficiency.

I write and record stuff: sometimes with some of my friends, but usually just on my own. Anything I'm particularly proud of and want to share with the world goes on my SoundCloud. My Last.fm is here. I like to think everything I listen to influences me in some way.

Coding

I really like computers and learning more about them. I'm interested in functional programming; right now I'm working on learning the Rust programming language. More on that in future posts!

I wrote a web app to help organize the musical recommendations I get from my friends. You can find it at http://erics-music-tracker.herokuapp.com/. Let me know what you think of it.
